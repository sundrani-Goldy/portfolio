Image and Video Generator

  • Tech Stack: Python, Django, SQlite, HTML, CSS, javascript,stable-diffusion, Protogen_V2.2(Pre-trained) model
  • Summary Page: Link

Our AI project aims to develop a smart system that transforms written words into both pictures and videos. To generate realistic images with written keywords and for video creation, the program will turn written stories into dynamic videos with features like smooth transitions between scenes. We're focused on user-friendliness, allowing people to easily input words, customize their preferences, and generate images or videos effortlessly. We plan to make the system adaptable and user-centric.
